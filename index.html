About Us

Seregar Biodegradables is a manufacturer of premium palm leaf tableware based in Karnataka, India. We use only naturally fallen palm leaves, hand-picked and steam-pressed into elegant plates, bowls, trays, and lunch boxes. Our mission is to reduce plastic waste by providing durable, eco-friendly alternatives for restaurants, hotels, and catering businesses worldwide.
